Welcome to Loja

The cultural and ecological city of Ecuador, known for its art and buildings of colonial architecture.

Once a year Ecuador lives a unique natural spectacle with the flowering of the guayacanes, dry forest trees located in few areas of the country that are dressed in intense gold, perfuming the environment and embellishing the landscape. The guayacán is a thousand year old tree, its beautiful and perfumed flowers can be contemplated of 3 to 6 days, in addition that its wood is considered one of the hardest and resistant of the American continent
